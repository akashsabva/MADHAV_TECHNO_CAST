Who We Are

Madhav Techno Cast – Trusted Casting Solutions

A fully-equipped B2B heavy engineering foundry based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, capable of handling single-piece castings from 20 kg to 2,500 kg.

Our facility runs Induction furnaces (500 kg–1,000 kg–2,500 kg single piece capacity), in-house pattern shop, CO₂ moulding, and spectrometer-based quality testing. Every casting is manufactured to IS, ASTM, and EN Series standards.

  • 24-Element In-house Spectrometer & NABL-Approved Testing
  • Double-Door Shot Blasting Machine & Heat Treatment
  • Automotive, Mining, Power, Sugar & Marine Sector Experience
  • Export-ready – near Mundra & Kandla Ports

How We Work

Manufacturing Process

From raw material to finished casting – every step controlled by qualified personnel.

01

Pattern Making

In-house and outsourced pattern shop with precise dimensional designs.

02

Moulding

CO₂, Sand Mullers, Silron, Sand Mixers & Molasses moulding methods.

03

Melting

Cupola (30" lining) & oil-fired furnaces for precise material melting.

04

Pouring

Precision pouring with real-time temperature and composition monitoring.

05

Finishing

Shot blasting, swing frame grinders & 5MT hoist cranes for clean finish.
