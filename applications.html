Industries We Serve

Applications Across Industries

Discover how Madhav Techno Cast delivers robust and precision-engineered casting components that power critical operations across various global industries.

Industrial Machines

Heavy industrial machinery requires components that can endure continuous operation, heavy loads, and extreme wear. We specialize in manufacturing high-strength and wear-resistant castings that form the backbone of industrial manufacturing equipment.

  • Typical Parts: Machine beds, gear housings, brackets, flywheels, and structural supports.
  • Materials: Carbon Steel, CI & MS Casting, Alloy Steel.
  • Key Benefit: Enhanced durability, excellent machinability, and vibration damping properties.

Automotive Sector

The automotive sector demands precision, consistency, and parts that can withstand dynamic stresses. We supply high-grade medium carbon and alloy steel wheels, hubs, and suspension components that ensure safety and performance in both passenger and commercial vehicles.

  • Typical Parts: EN8/EN9 Wheels, axle housings, suspension brackets, and transmission components.
  • Materials: EN Series Steel, Medium Carbon Steel.
  • Key Benefit: High tensile strength, excellent fatigue resistance, and rigorous dimensional accuracy.

Construction & Mining

Construction and earth-moving equipment operate in the harshest environments. To prevent costly downtime, we provide abrasion-resistant and impact-resistant castings specifically designed to handle massive structural loads and abrasive materials.

  • Typical Parts: Excavator bucket teeth, crusher jaws, links, track shoes, and heavy structural joints.
  • Materials: IS 1030 (Manganese Steel), High-Carbon Steel.
  • Key Benefit: Superior impact toughness, excellent work-hardening characteristics, and abrasion resistance.

General Engineering

For a vast array of general engineering applications, from pump housings to large gear blanks, versatility is key. Our foundry is equipped to handle complex geometries and custom metallurgical requirements from 20 kg up to 2,500 kg per piece.

  • Typical Parts: Valve bodies, pump casings, gear blanks, pulleys, and customized cast components.
  • Materials: Carbon Steel, CI & MS Castings.
  • Key Benefit: Custom engineering to precise drawing specifications with flawless internal soundness.

Power Plants & Energy

Power generation facilities require components that can sustain high temperatures, pressures, and corrosive environments over a long lifecycle. We deliver alloy and heat-resistant steel castings that ensure operational stability in thermal, hydro, and wind energy plants.

  • Typical Parts: Turbine casings, boiler components, heat exchanger parts, and high-pressure valves.
  • Materials: Heat Resistant Steel, Alloy Steel.
  • Key Benefit: Exceptional high-temperature strength, creep resistance, and thermal fatigue endurance.

Marine & Ports

The marine environment is highly corrosive and structurally demanding. Our stainless steel and specific alloy castings are manufactured to resist saltwater corrosion and immense maritime stresses, ensuring reliability for ships, docks, and offshore platforms.

  • Typical Parts: Propeller hubs, winches, bollards, anchors, and specialized deck hardware.
  • Materials: Stainless Steel (SS316/Duplex), specialized Alloy Steels.
  • Key Benefit: Superior pitting and corrosion resistance with robust mechanical integrity.

Sugar & Cement Mills

Heavy milling operations put continuous strain on their moving components. We provide heavy-duty castings that handle the heavy crushing, grinding, and material handling demands of cement and sugar processing plants without failure.

  • Typical Parts: Mill rollers, crushing gears, sprocket wheels, kiln tyres, and drag chain links.
  • Materials: IS 1030 Castings, Alloy Steel.
  • Key Benefit: High yield strength, extreme wear resistance, and long operational life under heavy loads.
