Who We Are

About Madhav Techno Cast

A fully-equipped B2B heavy engineering foundry in Ahmedabad, delivering precision castings from 20 kg to 2,500 kg for industries across India and the world.

Our Story

Built on Precision, Driven by Quality

Madhav Techno Cast was established with a single mission – to deliver high-quality metal castings that meet the demands of modern industry. Based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, we have grown into a trusted foundry serving both domestic and international markets.

Our facility handles everything in-house – from pattern making and moulding to melting, pouring, fettling, and quality testing. This end-to-end control allows us to maintain the consistency and standards our clients depend on.

We manufacture to IS, ASTM, and EN Series standards, making our castings suitable for procurement by domestic OEMs as well as overseas buyers from the USA, Germany, UAE, and Europe.
