Who We Are

Steel Casting & MS Casting Manufacturer in Ahmedabad – Madhav Techno Cast

A fully-equipped B2B heavy engineering steel casting foundry in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, delivering precision MS castings, Carbon Steel castings & Alloy Steel castings from 20 kg to 2,500 kg for industries across India, USA, Germany & UAE.

Our Story

Trusted Steel Casting & MS Casting Foundry – Built on Precision

Madhav Techno Cast was established with a single mission – to deliver high-quality steel castings and MS castings that meet the demands of modern industry. Based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, we have grown into a trusted steel casting manufacturer and MS casting supplier serving both domestic and international markets.

Our steel casting foundry handles everything in-house – from pattern making and moulding to induction furnace melting, precision pouring, fettling, shot blasting, and quality testing. This end-to-end control allows us to maintain the consistency and standards our clients depend on for every carbon steel casting, alloy steel casting, and CI & MS casting we produce.

We manufacture all steel castings and MS castings to IS, ASTM, and EN Series standards, making our castings suitable for procurement by domestic OEMs as well as overseas buyers from the USA, Germany, UAE, and Europe. Our induction furnace casting facility supports single-piece castings from 20 kg to 2,500 kg.

Frequently Asked Questions

Steel Casting & MS Casting – FAQs

Common questions about our steel casting, MS casting manufacturing, and foundry capabilities in Ahmedabad, Gujarat.

What types of steel castings does Madhav Techno Cast manufacture?

Madhav Techno Cast manufactures a wide range of steel castings including Carbon Steel Casting, Alloy Steel Casting, Manganese Steel Casting, MS (Mild Steel) Casting, CI (Cast Iron) Casting, Stainless Steel Casting, EN8/EN9 Steel Casting, IS 1030 Casting, and Heat Resistant Steel Casting. All castings are manufactured to IS, ASTM & EN standards in our Ahmedabad, Gujarat facility.

Where is your steel casting foundry located?

Our steel casting and MS casting manufacturing facility is located at 35, 36 Shivbhumi-5, Bakrol-Bujrang, Ahmedabad – 382433, Gujarat, India. We are strategically situated near Mundra Port (280 km) and Kandla Port (340 km) for efficient export logistics to USA, Germany, UAE, and worldwide.

What is your steel casting weight capacity?

Our induction furnace steel casting facility can produce single-piece castings from 20 kg to 2,500 kg. We operate 500 kg, 1,000 kg, and 2,500 kg capacity induction furnaces for precision steel casting and MS casting production at our Ahmedabad foundry.

Do you export steel castings internationally?

Yes, Madhav Techno Cast is an established steel casting exporter from India. We export steel castings and MS castings to USA, Germany, UAE, UK, Australia, and other countries. We provide ASTM standard castings for USA buyers and EN Series castings for European buyers with complete documentation including test certificates, mill certificates, and CoC.

What quality certifications do you hold for steel casting?

Madhav Techno Cast holds KAB-QC-81 certification from Pacatus Certification Pvt. Ltd. Our steel casting quality assurance includes in-house 24-element spectrometer analysis, NABL-approved testing laboratory, online temperature and CE measurement, and electronic hardness testing.

Which industries do you serve with steel castings?

We serve Automotive, Mining, Power Plants, Sugar & Cement Mills, Marine & Ports, Construction, and General Engineering industries with our precision steel castings and MS castings manufactured in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Our castings include gears, valve bodies, pump casings, machine beds, brackets, and custom components.
