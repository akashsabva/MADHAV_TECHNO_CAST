Understanding Mild Steel (MS) Grades

Mild steel is one of the most versatile and widely used materials in industrial casting and manufacturing. The strict control of chemical composition, especially Carbon (C), Manganese (Mn), and Silicon (Si), determines the mechanical properties, weldability, and structural integrity of the final cast component.

Common Industrial Applications

Mild steel grades offer excellent weldability and machinability, making them ideal for diverse applications.

Casting & Moulding

Used for casting heavy machinery beds, pump housings, and large industrial brackets where structural integrity is key.

Metal Fabrication

Highly favored for welding and fabrication works due to low carbon content preventing crack formations.

Machinery Parts

Ideal for general engineering components, shafts, base plates, and structural frames in manufacturing lines.

Structural Components

Used in construction, bridging, and heavy infrastructure projects as support beams and load-bearing structures.
