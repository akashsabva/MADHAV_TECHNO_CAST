Quality & Certifications

Stringent quality control at every process step – from raw material to finished casting. Certified. Traceable. Consistent.

Quality Policy

Quality with Consistency

Every casting manufactured at Madhav Techno Cast carries a unique identification mark on its un-machined surface. This enables precise traceability of the material used and process involved for every single piece.

All vendors and sub-contractors are selected after careful examination and test orders. Periodic and random checks are performed regularly to ensure consistent quality from every supplier.

Procedure-matrices are made for every process step and are strictly adhered to by our qualified metallurgical personnel – from moulding to fettling.

Material Traceability System

Each casting is stamped with a unique heat number on its un-machined surface at the time of production. This number links to:

  • Raw material heat number & supplier details
  • Chemical composition (spectrometer report)
  • Melt date, furnace record & pour temperature
  • Heat treatment batch & parameters
  • Final inspection report & hardness test results
Documents Available

Test Certificates & Reports

We provide complete documentation for every shipment, particularly important for international buyers requiring compliance documentation.

  • Mill Test Certificate (MTC) with chemical composition
  • Mechanical Test Report (Tensile, Hardness, Impact)
  • Certificate of Conformity (CoC) to IS / ASTM / EN
  • Radiographic / Ultrasonic Test Report (if specified)
  • Dimensional Inspection Report per drawing
