Carbon Steel Casting

Versatile castings in low, medium and high carbon grades for construction, manufacturing, and general industrial applications. IS 1030 / ASTM A27 compliant.

Carbon Steel Casting

Carbon steel castings are the most widely used category of steel castings. Their properties vary with carbon content – low carbon for ductility and weldability, medium carbon for balanced strength-toughness, and high carbon for hardness and wear resistance.

Our carbon steel castings conform to IS 1030 and ASTM A27/A216 standards. We produce castings across the full carbon steel range using Cupola and oil-fired furnaces. Chemistry is verified using our in-house spectrometer before every pour. Heat treatment is offered as per specification – annealing, normalizing, or quench and temper – to meet mechanical property requirements.

Key Features

  • Three grades: Low (0.3% C), Medium (0.35 – 0.6% C), High (> 0.6% C) carbon
  • Standards: IS 1030 / ASTM A27 / ASTM A216 WCB
  • Spectrometer verified chemistry on every heat
  • Heat treatment as per specification
  • 20 kg to 2,500 kg capacity
