Heat Resistant Steel Casting

High-temperature steel castings for furnace components, kiln parts and elevated-temperature industrial applications. ASTM A297 (HH, HK grades), up to 1100°C service.

Madhav Techno Cast manufactures these castings conforming to ASTM A297 (HH, HK, HP) / IS Heat Resistant grades. All castings are produced with spectrometer-verified chemistry and appropriate heat treatment. Capacity: 20 kg to 2,500 kg. Test certificates (MTC, CoC) provided with every order.

Applications

Furnace Trays & BasketsKiln Cars & FurnitureCement Kiln PartsHeat Treatment FixturesPower Plant PartsPetrochemical Components
