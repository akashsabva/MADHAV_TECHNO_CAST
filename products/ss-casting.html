SS Casting

Premium grade Stainless Steel castings including SS304, SS304L, SS316, SS316L, and Duplex alloys designed for exceptional durability and corrosion resistance.

Premium SS Casting Solutions

We specialize in manufacturing high-quality Stainless Steel castings that cater to demanding industrial applications requiring superior corrosion resistance, high strength, and longevity.

Our SS casting range includes widely used grades such as SS304, SS304L, SS316, SS316L, and Duplex stainless steel. Each grade is selected based on its unique chemical composition and mechanical properties, ensuring optimal performance in specific operational environments. From food processing and chemical plants to marine and pharmaceutical equipment, our precision castings are engineered to meet strict international quality standards.

Available SS Grades

  • SS304 (CF8): Excellent corrosion resistance, ideal for general industrial and food processing applications.
  • SS304L (CF3): Low carbon version of 304, offering enhanced weldability and reduced risk of carbide precipitation.
  • SS316 (CF8M): Contains molybdenum for improved resistance to pitting and crevice corrosion, suitable for marine and chemical environments.
  • SS316L (CF3M): Low carbon version of 316, combining superior corrosion resistance with excellent welding properties.
  • Duplex: Offers a combination of high strength and exceptional stress-corrosion cracking resistance, perfect for heavy-duty applications.
