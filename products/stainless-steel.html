Stainless Steel Casting

Corrosion-resistant castings in SS304 (CF8) and SS316 (CF8M) grades for food, chemical, and marine industries. ASTM A743 / A351 compliant.

Madhav Techno Cast manufactures these castings conforming to ASTM A743 / A351 / A744 (CF8, CF8M, CN7M). All castings are produced with spectrometer-verified chemistry and appropriate heat treatment. Capacity: 20 kg to 2,500 kg. Test certificates (MTC, CoC) provided with every order.

Applications

Chemical Process PumpsPharmaceutical EquipmentMarine ComponentsFood Industry PartsCorrosion-Resistant ValvesHeat Exchanger Parts
