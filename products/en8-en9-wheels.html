EN8 / EN9 Steel Wheel Casting

EN8 and EN9 grade steel castings are widely used in automotive and general engineering applications where medium-to-high tensile strength, good machinability, and wear resistance are required.

Madhav Techno Cast manufactures EN8/EN9 wheel castings conforming to IS 1570 and EN standards. These castings are produced in our Cupola and oil-fired furnaces, followed by heat treatment (normalizing/hardening-tempering) to achieve the specified mechanical properties. Each casting is uniquely identified for full traceability.

Key Features & Capabilities

  • Medium-to-high carbon steel with good machinability
  • Tensile strength: EN8 – 620 MPa; EN9 – 700 MPa (min.)
  • Heat treatment: Normalizing, Hardening & Tempering available
  • Single piece weight capacity: 20 kg to 2,500 kg
  • Unique heat identification for full traceability
  • Test certificates (MTC, CoC) provided with every order

Applications

Automotive Components Railway Wheels & Bogies Shafts & Axles General Engineering Machine Tool Parts Construction Equipment
