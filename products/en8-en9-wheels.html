EN Series Steel Casting

EN Series steel castings (EN8, EN9, EN19, EN24) are widely used in automotive and general engineering applications where medium-to-high tensile strength, good machinability, and wear resistance are required.

Madhav Techno Cast manufactures EN Series steel castings conforming to IS 1570 and EN standards. These castings are produced in our Cupola and oil-fired furnaces, followed by heat treatment (normalizing/hardening-tempering) to achieve the specified mechanical properties. Each casting is uniquely identified for full traceability.

Key Features & Capabilities

  • Medium-to-high carbon steel with good machinability
  • Tensile strength: EN8 – 620 MPa; EN9 – 700 MPa; EN19 – 850 MPa; EN24 – 930 MPa (min.)
  • Heat treatment: Normalizing, Hardening & Tempering available
  • Single piece weight capacity: 20 kg to 2,500 kg
  • Unique heat identification for full traceability
  • Test certificates (MTC, CoC) provided with every order

Applications

Automotive Components Railway Wheels & Bogies Shafts & Axles General Engineering Machine Tool Parts Construction Equipment
