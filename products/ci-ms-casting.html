CI & MS Casting

Cast Iron (CI) and Mild Steel (MS) castings – our core expertise. IS 210 / IS 1030 compliant. Machine beds, brackets, housings, and industrial components. 20 kg to 2,500 kg.

Madhav Techno Cast manufactures these castings conforming to IS 210 (FG150–FG350) for CI; IS 1030 for MS. All castings are produced with spectrometer-verified chemistry and appropriate heat treatment. Capacity: 20 kg to 2,500 kg. Test certificates (MTC, CoC) provided with every order.

Applications

Machine Beds & FramesGear HousingsBrackets & SupportsIndustrial ComponentsPump CasingsInfrastructure Parts
