Alloy Steel Casting

Enhanced mechanical properties for structural, industrial, and power generation applications. IS 2644 / ASTM A148 compliant.

Alloy Steel Casting

Alloy steel castings incorporate specific alloying elements such as Nickel (Ni), Chromium (Cr), Molybdenum (Mo), and Vanadium (V) to achieve superior strength, toughness, and hardenability compared to plain carbon steel.

Madhav Techno Cast manufactures alloy steel castings conforming to IS 2644 and ASTM A148 standards. These castings are produced with precise chemistry control using our in-house 24-element spectrometer, followed by appropriate heat treatment to achieve the required mechanical properties. Custom alloy grades are available as per customer specifications and application drawings.

Key Features

  • Alloying elements: Ni, Cr, Mo, V for enhanced properties
  • Tensile strength: 620–1200 MPa depending on grade & heat treatment
  • Full spectrometer analysis for every heat
  • Heat treatment: Normalize, Q&T available
  • Capacity: 20 kg to 2,500 kg per piece
  • MTC and CoC provided with every order

Applications

Pump & Valve CastingsGear & Gearbox PartsPower Plant ComponentsPressure Vessel PartsStructural CastingsMarine Components
